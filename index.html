Hello

We are the Crypto Club at Seattle University trying to dispell myths about digital assets and bring awareness to the cultural revolution happening beneath our noses.

Our Goals.

Address Concerns about Cryptocurrencies

People like to throw cryptocurrencies under the bus and dismiss it as a fad by making it seem like crypto is dangerous. We would like to show you that it is not and how crypto is fair, transparent, and equitable

Onboarding people to Use Web 3.0 and cryptocurrencies

Learning how blockchains work and how to get started in investing in crypto is a lot of hard work. Let us help with that.

Condensing all the information in the Crypto World into byte-sized readings

Crypto is innovating at lightspeed. Developers and blockchains releasing new features and decentralized applications for the masses can be confusing and time consuming. Let us do the pruning for curated crypto news.
